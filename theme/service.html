Manufacturing Procedures:

• Hi-tech manufacturing facility along with a separate, robust and scalable set up to meet national and international project specifications.

• Facilities located in the outskirts of Mumbai. The facilities have a perfected and matching synergy with an experienced human resource pool.

• Systems and procedures with the latest national, international standards and codes to meet the demanding technical needs of the customer.

• Manufacturing company working on structure of ‘Lean Manufacturing’ and the entire setup has been streamlined for a smooth production flow based on a pull system.

• R & D center for continues technological development in the existing range of products and to add new products for complete turnkey solutions for our valued customers.

Manufacturing Facilities:

• Equipped with CNC and VMC machines to meet on time delivery.

• Painting Facility to meet international project specified requirement.

• In-house machinery setup to meet quality policy and timely deliveries.

• Systematically organized assembly and testing section to reduce assembly time and meet our quality standards.

Quality Control:

• Equipped with Go-No-Go gauges, fixtures and mandrils for quality check of machined components.

• Special measuring instruments and Gear Tooth Vernier Calipers to check the pitch and module of gears.

• Height gauge with Calibrated Surface table for R&D of new products.

• Hardness Tester for inspection of incoming raw materials.

Testing Facilities:

• Our Manufacturing facility is well equipped with Torque Testing Machine for measuring output torque values of Gearboxes.

• Specially designed Life cycle testing bench, to test and calculate the life cycle of Gearboxes.

• Torque testing bench to measure rated output torque, I/P Thrust and various other parameters required to calculate the efficiency and performance of the gearboxes.
