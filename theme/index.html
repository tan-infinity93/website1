COMPANY PROFILE:

> Transtork Automation Private Limited is a company involved in manufacturing of Gear Boxes that are used with valve, dampers and most other quarter-turn applications to meet the diverse Automation demands of the modern process industries.

> The company has an engineering and technical expertise together with the facilities to address the diverse automation demands of the modern process industries. Standard, Special and Customized Gearboxes are all available on demand for use in a wide range of applications and industries.

> Transtork Gearboxes are produced in strict compliance with the Quality Management System requirement and in conformance with the engineering codes in practice and relevant standards.

> Our attention to quality at every level of the manufacturing process, rigorous approval standards for material sourcing, preproduction and postproduction inspections, ISO certified, randomly selected measures and tested operator’s leads to the products that are backed by our commitment to customer needs.

VISION:

> To become “Customers Preferred Company” worldwide, that Manufactures wide range of Quarter turn, Full turn and Bevel Gearboxes and other Automation accessories including large sizes and customizations as per customer’s requirement with Global Service Support, Zero Defects within the shortest lead time and several other services.

MISSION:

> Empowering Gear Technology to meet the diverse Automation demands of modern process industries, strengthen internal operating process, streamline the vendor base and establish pro-active planning mechanism to support the establishment of sales and services globally.

QUALITY ASSURANCE:

Our Quality Management System Complies with ISO 9001:2015. We follow National and International Standards in strict compliance to the Quality Management Systems Requirement in conformance with the engineering codes practiced in accordance to their relevant standards. We ensure that all our employees are trained and are aware of Customers expectation and requirement for higher degree of accuracy and a better quality product.
