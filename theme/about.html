About Transtork Automation Pvt. Ltd

Empowering Gear Technology

Company Introduction:

> Transtork Automation Private Limited is a company that is engaged in the Design, Development and Manufacturing of comprehensive range of Gear Boxes that are used with valves and dampers, as well as most other quarter-turn applications and other associated industries.

> The company has the engineering and technical expertise together with the facilities to address the diverse automation demands of the modern process industries.

>

Manufacturing:

> Our manufacturing unit is equipped with latest machineries, equipment’s and testing facilities.

> Our production team of skilled workers ensures to employ latest state-of-the-art-of modern techniques in the entire cycle of production under stringent quality control procedures.

> The gearboxes and their components are manufactured to the highest degree of accuracy to ensure a trouble free long life and guaranteed interchangeability of spares.

Design and Engineering:

> Our Engineering department’s mission is Empowering Gear Technology and designing innovations to meet the diverse Automation demands of the modern process industries.

> Our ability, experience and expertise through the use of sophisticated technology and CAD/CAE/3D equipment results in the production of high quality gearboxes for wide range of applications.

Quality Policy:

> Our Gear Boxes are produced in strict compliance with the Quality Management System Requirements and in conformance with the engineering codes in practice and relevant standards.

Customer Service:

> We have fully integrated customer service division, which is fully geared to react speedily to all customer’s enquiries and whatever technical support needed.

Time Management:

> At Transtork, we emphasize on the effective time management policy while dealing with our customers. This policy enables us to adhere to upmost product quality, fulfilling customer requirements and providing timely deliveries.
